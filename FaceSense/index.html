FaceSense -- fast and accurate facial information on your platform.

FaceSense is the next generation facial feature and analysis toolkit from RealD ME. It is especially tuned towards extremely fast execution, multi target tracking and analysis of head and facial motions.

A demonstrator application of FaceSense is now available for the following platforms:

  • App store
  • Play Store
  • Windows Store
  • JS Javascript

Extremely fast

Optimized algorithmically and tuned to your platform, FaceSense yields results in milliseconds. No GPU or server needed!

Mobile first

Built on custom dataset with mobile users in mind, the tracker works in the most adversary conditions.

Well documented.

FaceSense comes with extensive documentation and examples for its Java, Python, C++ and Javascript API.

Constant time.

Total predictability for your image processing pipeline.

Focus on the user

Communication

Facial feature tracking is increasingly forming the basis of communication between devices and their users -- with all kinds of applications including measuring audience reactions, AR/VR, animaiton, security, and many others.

User-Device Interaction

Gaze direction and emotion classification allows vendors to understand user feelings and provide for customizable experience.

Groups

RealDMe FaceSense tracks multiple people at camera framerates with multiple annotations. The system follows multiple persons with wide ranging poses and scales, allowing robust estimation of interaction between users, their focus of attention and emotion.

Performance powered by RealDMe

RealDMe FaceSense runs in real-time and at low-power due to algorithmic and device specific tuning. RealDMe offers fine tuning of the algorithm to your platform (GPU, vectorization extension, branch predictions) if your platform is not directly targeted or you want to tune FaceSense to your use case.

Amazing Compatibility.

FaceSense comes with a C, Java and Python API. FaceSense runs on a wide variety of platforms: iOS, Android, Windows, MacOS, Linux.
